About Us

More than a dispensary—
a community spot.

The Grass Station is located at 8049 Highway 4 in Paqtnkek Mi'kmaw Nation, right outside Antigonish. We're an Indigenous-owned cannabis retailer that's been serving this community with quality, government-licensed products in a space where everyone feels welcome.

Whether you're a regular or walking in for the first time, our staff knows their stuff and will help you find exactly what you're looking for. No pressure, no judgment.
